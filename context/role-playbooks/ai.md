# AI Role Playbook

## Target positioning

AI Systems Engineer focused on agent workflows, reliable orchestration, and high-impact automation.

## Prioritize these signals

- E-001 latency reduction and pipeline impact.
- E-002 human-in-the-loop reliability controls.
- E-006 AI onboarding transformation.
- E-003 as production-scale credibility.

## Secondary signals

- E-004 for delivery rigor.
- E-007/E-008 for systems-depth differentiation.

## De-prioritize

- Generic frontend-heavy statements without AI relevance.
- Tool lists that are not tied to outcomes.

## Recommended summary shape

- Line 1: AI systems focus + production context.
- Line 2: measurable improvement claim.
- Line 3: reliability/cost-aware execution orientation.

1) LangChain, LangGraph, agent workflows
2) Python, TypeScript, Node.js
