# Frontend Role Playbook

## Target positioning

Product-focused engineer with end-to-end ownership and ability to translate user-facing flows into reliable systems-backed experiences.

## Prioritize these signals

- E-006 onboarding flow transformation.
- E-003 cross-functional full-stack execution at scale.
- E-001 workflow simplification and user-facing throughput impact.

## Secondary signals

- E-004 delivery discipline for rapid iteration.
- Selective mention of AI-assisted product experiences.

## De-prioritize

- Deep infra internals unless JD asks for them.
- Systems/kernel details unless framed as engineering rigor.

## Recommended summary shape

- Line 1: product and user-flow orientation.
- Line 2: data-to-experience conversion strength.
- Line 3: full-stack collaboration and delivery speed.

## Skills emphasis order

1) TypeScript, frontend frameworks used in production
2) API integration and backend collaboration
3) product analytics/iteration mindset
4) cloud/devops only as supporting signal

## Notes

Frontend evidence in current profile is lighter than AI/DevOps/backend evidence. For strong frontend JDs, bias toward full-stack framing and user-flow outcomes.
