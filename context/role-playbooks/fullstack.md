# Full Stack Role Playbook

## Target positioning

Full-stack engineer who can design, build, and operate production systems across application, backend, and cloud layers.

## Prioritize these signals

- E-003 end-to-end platform growth and architecture ownership.
- E-006 user input to structured workflow automation.
- E-004 CI/CD and deployment ownership.
- E-001 as high-impact optimization proof.

## Secondary signals

- E-005 operational scaling.
- E-002 reliability controls in intelligent workflows.

## De-prioritize

- Overly narrow specialist framing unless JD requires it.
- Skill lists that are not tied to delivered outcomes.

## Recommended summary shape

- Line 1: full-stack ownership across product and platform.
- Line 2: measurable outcomes and scalability.
- Line 3: ability to ship quickly with production quality.

## Skills emphasis order

1) TypeScript, Node.js, service architecture
2) cloud deployment and CI/CD
3) AI workflow integration where relevant
4) databases and reliability practices
